Emerson Xia / Info


Emerson Xia (b. 2003, San Francisco) is a first-generation Asian American artist and photographer living in Minnesota. He works primarily with photography and finds inspiration through his own experience and interaction with the natural world. He explores the theme of masculinity and looking at the world without fear. Currently a junior at St. Olaf College in Northfield, Minnesota.
